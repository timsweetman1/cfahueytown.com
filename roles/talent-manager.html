About the opportunity

Chick-fil-A Hueytown is opening in 2026, and we're hiring the leader who will build our people and training systems from the ground up. This is a founding-team role — not a backfill, not an inherited program. You will design how we hire, how we onboard, how we train, and how we develop the 80+ team members who will define this restaurant for years to come.

If you've built a training program from scratch (not just delivered one), if you hire for character before credentials, and if you believe the right people in the right seats is what makes a business extraordinary — keep reading.

What you'll own

  • Training program design & delivery. Build the full curriculum from zero — onboarding, systems training, service standards, recertification, leadership development. Lead the grand opening training sprint that takes ~80 people from hired to ready in 6–7 weeks. Train trainers, not just trainees.
  • Talent acquisition. Build and run the recruiting engine — sourcing, screening, interviewing, and selecting for attitude and relationship instinct. Lead the hiring sprint to bring on ~80 founding team members, then maintain a perpetual pipeline after opening.
  • Onboarding & first impressions. Design the experience that makes every new hire feel welcomed, prepared, and clear on expectations from day one.
  • Team member & leader development. Create the career pathway from Team Member → Trainer → Team Leader → Manager → Director. Identify high-potential talent early and invest deliberately.
  • Retention & employee experience. Own morale, recognition, team events, and the small acts of care that make people stay.
  • Culture stewardship. Be a primary carrier of the Hueytown Method — our operational philosophy built on core values — and help write the playbook as we grow.

What you bring

Required:

  • Direct experience designing and delivering training programs at scale — not just participating or coordinating
  • Experience running structured hiring processes — screening, interviewing, and selecting for character
  • Proven ability to lead groups of 30+ through training or onboarding
  • Strong organizational ability — you've managed complex, multi-variable programs under time pressure
  • Availability for a variable schedule, including hiring events, grand opening, and team activities

Preferred:

  • You've built a curriculum from scratch, not inherited one
  • Background in high-volume events, hospitality, or corporate logistics
  • Familiarity with Chick-fil-A systems, Pathway, or corporate development resources
  • Ties to the Hueytown or Birmingham metro community
  • Exposure to Topgrading, Working Genius, Five Voices, or similar development frameworks
  • Bachelor's degree in HR, business, psychology, education, or a related field

What success looks like

  • Grand opening team of ~80 hired, trained, and operationally ready by launch
  • Training program documented and repeatable — not dependent on any one person
  • New hire retention at 90 days exceeds industry benchmarks
  • Team members can articulate and demonstrate the Hueytown Method
  • A visible leadership pipeline in motion within the first 6 months

Growth path

This role is built to expand with the right person — leadership of a talent and training team, Director designation for whole-business impact, cross-functional opportunities, and Operator Development Program eligibility for candidates with long-term entrepreneurial goals.

Who thrives here

People skills are necessary but not sufficient. We hire on character first. The person who thrives here walks into a room of 40 new hires and immediately thinks about how to design an experience that makes every one of them better. They hire on gut and relationship instinct, then build the systems to develop what they saw in the interview. They're as comfortable in a logistics conversation as a coaching conversation.

Chick-fil-A Hueytown is an equal opportunity employer committed to building a team that reflects the community we serve.

We'd love to hear from you. This is a founding team — the people who join now help set the standard for everything that comes after.
